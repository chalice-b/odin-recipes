Blueberry Flax Pancakes

Description

A fast recipe for blueberry flax pancakes. It takes around 10 minutes to make and this particular recipe is for 4 servings. The outcome should be a fluffy pancake filled with nutritious fiber.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix
  • 1/2 cup flax seed meal
  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup fresh or thawed frozen blueberries

Steps

  1. Set a nonstick skillet over medium heat
  2. In a bowl, stir the pancake mix and flax seed meal together
  3. In a measuring cup, whisk milk and eggs together
  4. Pour the liquid into the bowl
  5. Stir just until moistened
  6. Spoon 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the skillet
  7. Sprinkle as many blueberries as wanted
  8. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip
  9. Cook until browned on the other side
